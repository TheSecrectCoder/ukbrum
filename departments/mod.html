About Ministry of Defence Police

The Ministry of Defence Police (MDP) in UKBRUM is a specialized police force responsible for providing armed security and counter-terrorism capabilities for the UK's defense infrastructure. Our officers are tasked with protecting critical national assets and supporting national security operations.

As a member of the MDP, you'll participate in high-level security operations, escort duties, and specialized policing services. We emphasize discipline, attention to detail, and adherence to strict security protocols.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide armed security for defense establishments
  • Conduct counter-terrorism operations
  • Escort high-value assets and personnel
  • Investigate crimes related to defense infrastructure
  • Coordinate with other security agencies during major incidents
