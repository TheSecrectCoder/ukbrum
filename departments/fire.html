About West Midlands Fire Service

The West Midlands Fire Service (WMFS) in UKBRUM is dedicated to protecting the community from fire and other emergencies. Our firefighters respond to a wide range of incidents, from structure fires to complex rescue operations, while also focusing on prevention through community education.

As a member of WMFS, you'll participate in realistic emergency response scenarios, training exercises, and community outreach programs. We emphasize teamwork, physical fitness, and technical expertise in all aspects of firefighting and rescue operations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Respond to fire emergencies and suppress fires
  • Conduct technical rescue operations
  • Provide emergency medical assistance
  • Perform fire prevention inspections
  • Educate the community on fire safety
